Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Hafiz Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and while using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our services. By using our app or website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

We believe in privacy by design. We do not collect any personal data that can identify you directly (such as your name, email address, or phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., via support requests).

Usage Data & Analytics

To improve our application, we may collect anonymous usage data. This data helps us understand how the app is used, identify bugs, and improve performance. This information is aggregated and does not identify individual users personally.

Local Storage

All your progress, settings, and downloaded content (such as audio files) are stored locally on your device. We do not sync this data to any cloud servers unless explicitly stated or if you use platform-specific backup features (like iCloud or Google Drive backups managed by your OS).

App Permissions

The Hafiz Pro app requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Internet Access: Required to download audio recitations and check for app updates.
  • Storage: Required to save downloaded audio files and your progress locally on your device so you can use the app offline.
  • Audio/Microphone: Although primarily for listening, future features involving recitation testing may request microphone access. This data is processed locally and is not uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use open-source libraries and APIs to provide content, such as:

  • Quran API: To fetch Quranic text and audio metadata.
  • Audio Reciters: Audio files are streamed or downloaded from reputable Islamic sources.

We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or marketing agencies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
